About Water Test Kits

SDI (Silt Density Index) kits measure RO feed water fouling potential; hardness kits check softener output. Simple, field-usable kits with consumable papers/reagents.

Where are they used?

RO commissioning and monitoring, softener checks and water quality verification. Ask for refill papers and reagents too.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is SDI and why test it?

SDI shows how quickly water fouls a membrane; high SDI needs better pre-treatment. WhatsApp 9899193589 for the kit. Prices exclude GST.

How do I test water hardness?

A drop-count titration kit gives hardness in ppm to check softener performance.

Do you supply refill papers/reagents?

Yes, SDI filter papers and hardness reagents are available separately.
